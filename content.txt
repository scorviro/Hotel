ABOUT US PAGE
Our Story
Page Title H1: The Blackstone Story - Built on Bold Ambitions
BRAND NARRATIVE
In 2015, a vision was set in stone - quite literally. The Blackstone Hotel was conceived as Rajkot's answer to a hospitality gap: a property that could serve the business traveller with the infrastructure of a corporate hotel while delivering the warmth and aesthetic of a boutique luxury escape. A decade later, that vision has been realised across every tile, every pendant light, and every plate that leaves our kitchen.
Our name is not metaphor. The Blackstone identity is built into our architecture - the deep black marble that dominates our reception, the contrast of dark and gold that defines our brand, the weight and permanence of a hotel that intends to stand for generations. Located close to the Rajkot GIDC and the Ahmedabad bypass, we are positioned at the intersection of commerce and culture, accessibility and aspiration.
Our Philosophy
Philosophy Headline: Luxury Is Not What You Add. It Is What You Leave Out.
At The Blackstone, we believe in the luxury of restraint. Our rooms do not shout. Our restaurant does not overwhelm. Our service does not intrude. Instead, we have created spaces where quality speaks in a quiet voice - where the excellence reveals itself over time, in the weight of a door handle, the angle of a reading light, the temperature of your morning coffee before you have asked.
Heritage Wall - Maharaja Reference
The portrait of Maharaja Krishnakumarsinhji of Bhavnagar that presides over our reception is not decoration. It is a declaration of our roots in Gujarat's royal legacy of hospitality - the tradition that says a guest is God (Atithi Devo Bhava). We honour that tradition every day, in every interaction, without exception.

THE ONE-IN-A-MILLION NAVBAR
DESIGN CONCEPT
'The Celestial Progress Bar' - A Navbar That Tells the Hotel Story as You Scroll
The navbar is not a navigation bar. It is a cinematic progress instrument. As the user scrolls through the 13 sections of the website - each mapped to the 1,073 frame walkthrough - the navbar transforms live to show exactly where in the hotel's story the user is standing.
Visual Structure
Position: Fixed top, full width, 72px height
Background: Transparent on hero ? black (rgba 0,0,0,0.92) with gold blur backdrop-filter after scroll threshold
Logo: Centre-aligned gold lotus SVG logo. Scales: 48px on load ? 38px after scroll.
Navigation Links: Left-aligned: Home / About / Rooms / Restaurant / Banquet / Gallery / Contact
CTA Button: Right-aligned: 'Book Now' - gold border, transparent fill ? gold fill on hover
The Unique Feature: Frame Progress Arc
Below the standard navbar elements, a 2px gold progress line spans the full width - but it is not a simple scroll indicator. It is segmented into 13 colour-coded zones (one per website section). The active zone pulses with a warm gold glow. The completed zones become solid dark gold. The upcoming zones are ghost-white at 20% opacity.
On hover over any navbar link, a micro-preview tooltip appears above the link - showing a small thumbnail (from the walkthrough frames) of the section that link leads to. This is a hotel industry first.
Frame Counter Element
A discreet element in the upper-right corner of the navbar shows: 'Frame 001 / 1073' - updating in real time as the user scrolls, mirroring the video's own frame counter. This creates an extraordinary sense of cinematographic immersion.
Font: Frame counter uses monospace (Courier New) in gold at 11px - deliberately cinematic
